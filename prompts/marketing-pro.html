#1Quarterly Content Calendar with Pillar-Cluster Architecture+
You are a senior content strategist with 15 years of experience in {industry}. Build a comprehensive 90-day content calendar for {brand_name}, a {business_type} targeting {target_audience}. Context: - Primary business goals this quarter: {quarterly_goals} - Top 3 product/service lines to promote: {products_or_services} - Competitor content gaps we've identified: {competitor_gaps} - Available content formats: {available_formats, e.g., blog, video, podcast, infographic} - Publishing capacity: {posts_per_week} posts per week across {number_of_channels} channels Requirements: 1. Define 3-4 content pillars that align with business goals 2. For each pillar, create a topic cluster with 1 pillar page and 6-8 cluster articles 3. Map each piece to a buyer journey stage (Awareness, Consideration, Decision) 4. Include content repurposing chains (blog -> social carousel -> email -> video script) 5. Flag 5 "tentpole" content pieces that drive outsized results 6. Include seasonal hooks, industry events, and trending topic slots 7. Assign a primary KPI to each content piece Output Format: - Month-by-month table: Week | Content Title | Pillar | Format | Channel | Buyer Stage | KPI | Repurposing Chain - Executive summary paragraph - Content production timeline with deadlines and dependencies
Tip: Feed the AI your Google Analytics top pages and Search Console queries first. The more real data you provide about what already works, the sharper the calendar will be.
